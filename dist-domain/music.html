The Songs of Riley Green — Stories Behind the Music

What separates Riley Green's catalogue from the majority of mainstream country is that every song he has released feels like it was written because he had no choice — because the truth of it needed to come out. There is no album filler in his discography. There are only degrees of honesty.

"I Wish Grandpas Never Died" — The Song That Defined a Generation of Country Fans

No Riley Green song has connected with listeners the way "I Wish Grandpas Never Died" has. Written as a direct tribute to his grandfather Melvyn Green, the song captures a kind of grief that most country music doesn't have the courage to address directly — the ordinary, devastating loss of someone who taught you everything important. The song was nearly left off the album after the label called it "too simple." It became one of the most emotionally resonant country songs of its decade. If you search for Riley Green songs, this is where most people start.

"Miles on It" — Riley Green's Biggest Commercial Moment

"Miles on It" — recorded with Ella Langley — became Riley Green's first #1 hit on the Billboard Country Airplay chart in 2023. The song is a departure from the introspective, solitary quality of much of his earlier work, leaning into the shared experience of falling for someone. Ella Langley's voice alongside Riley's created a pairing that felt natural and inevitable. The music video accumulated tens of millions of views, introducing Riley Green to an entirely new audience that had not yet found "I Wish Grandpas Never Died."

"Different 'Round Here" feat. Luke Combs

The title track of Riley Green's debut album became his signature song before he had a signature sound. With Luke Combs featuring on the track, the song reached an audience far beyond what a debut artist would normally command. The collaboration felt genuine rather than calculated — two artists who came from the same place in country music finding common ground in a song about the particular pride of being from somewhere most people don't know.

"There Was This Girl" — The Beginning

Riley Green's debut single was released in 2018 and peaked at #4 on the Billboard Country Airplay chart — an extraordinary debut that announced a voice with commercial instincts and genuine emotional weight in equal measure. "There Was This Girl" is a breakup song written the way breakup songs rarely are — with more wistfulness than bitterness, more honesty than performance.

Where to Listen to Riley Green Songs

Riley Green's full catalogue is available on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and Amazon Music. His most-streamed songs include "I Wish Grandpas Never Died," "Miles on It," "Different 'Round Here," "There Was This Girl," and "Way Out Here."
